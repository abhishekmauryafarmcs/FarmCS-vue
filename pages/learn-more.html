Revolutionizing Precision Agriculture with Sinchita

FarmCS introduces Sinchita - an AI-powered autonomous mobile rover designed to revolutionize crop monitoring and disease detection. By combining advanced machine learning, real-time field intelligence, and autonomous navigation, Sinchita empowers farmers to detect diseases early, optimize crop management, and maximize yields while minimizing crop loss.

Our Vision

Our vision is to transform agriculture by providing every farmer with cutting-edge AI-driven field intelligence, enabling them to increase yields by 85-95%, reduce crop loss by 30-40%, and build a sustainable, food-secure future for India.

The Challenge

Tomato farmers face critical challenges: lack of real-time visibility into crop health, manual scouting that misses early disease symptoms, and 30-40% yield loss due to undetected diseases. Static sensors fail to provide plant-level insights. Sinchita addresses these challenges by autonomously monitoring every plant with HD imaging and AI-powered disease detection.
