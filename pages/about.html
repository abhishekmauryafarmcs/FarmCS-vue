Our Mission

To empower Indian farmers with AI-driven field intelligence through Sinchita - an autonomous rover that detects crop diseases early, monitors plant health in real-time, and delivers actionable insights for sustainable, high-yield farming.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every farmer has access to cutting-edge precision agriculture technology, enabling them to increase yields by 85-95%, reduce crop loss by 30-40%, and build a sustainable food-secure India.

What Makes Sinchita Different

AI-powered autonomous monitoring, real-time disease detection, and intelligent recommendations.

Autonomous Mobile Rover

Moves & monitors every plant with HD crop imaging for comprehensive field coverage without human intervention.

DenseNet AI Model

Advanced deep learning predicts diseases before visible damage appears with 10-category tomato disease detection.

Early Blight & Septoria Detection

Identifies Early Blight, Septoria, and other tomato diseases at the earliest stages for immediate intervention.

Smart Recommendations

Intelligent treatment & fertigation suggestions based on real-time field data and disease analysis.

Adaptive Height Design

Scalable height adjustment for different growth stages ensuring optimal monitoring throughout crop lifecycle.

LoRaWan IoT Communication

Real-time data transmission with long-range wireless connectivity for remote field monitoring.
